FireHouse is a stone's throw away from iconic New York City landmark.

Combining the vibrant energy of New York City with an authentic classic setting in Midtown.

  • The 130-seat dining room and upper level private dining room offer a chic and elegant backdrop for an unforgettable dining experience.
  • FireHouse's impressively high ceilings, mahogany wood walls, and dark leather tufted banquets create a rich yet inviting atmosphere.
  • Behind the grand wooden door entrance, is an oak wood bar followed by a corridor lined with oversized circular banquettes.

We're proud to have received Trip Advisor's 2020 "Travelers' Choice Best of the Best Award" ranked amongst the top 10% around the world, as well as Wine Spectator's Award of Excellence for offering a more extensive wine selections with significant vintage depth and excellent breadth across multiple regions.
